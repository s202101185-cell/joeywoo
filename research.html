Research

My work constructs explicit examples for cohesion + quantum modalities and studies obstructions to certain coreflective structures.

Core themes

  • Explicit cohesive models using finite-dimensional operator algebras.
  • Monoidal comonads and quantum modalities.
  • Obstruction theorems and constructive resolutions.

Approach

We prefer examples that can be computed and examined closely, exposing subtle obstructions that abstract statements hide.
