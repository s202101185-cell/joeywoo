Research

My work gives explicit constructions that illustrate the interaction between cohesion and quantum modalities and investigates structural obstructions and possible resolutions.

Core themes

  • Concrete models of cohesive ∞‑topoi using finite-dimensional algebras.
  • Monoidal comonads and quantum modalities: structure and degeneracy.
  • Obstruction theorems and constructive pathways to resolution.

Approach

The emphasis is on explicit examples and categorical clarity — to make abstract ideas usable in computation and clear in semantics.
