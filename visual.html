Visual Explorations

Geometric intuition for cohesive ∞‑topoi, quantum modalities, and the obstruction theorem.

1D – Gelfand spectrum collapse. Points on the line represent characters of a commutative C*-algebra; under the centre comonad, the spectrum collapses to a single point (the trivial character).

2D – Precomposition obstruction. The diagram illustrates how the diagonal map Δ fails to commute with Day convolution, collapsing the multiplicative conjunction to the cartesian product.

3D – Quantum site. The grid of spheres represents geometric manifolds; vertical columns are algebraic Hilbert spaces. Click Apply Q to see the quantum modality project onto the commutative core.

4D – Distributive law coherence. A rotating tesseract encodes the mixed distributive law. Coloured edges trace the entwining map ψ : g ⊗ δₓ → δ_{gxg⁻¹} ⊗ g.
